Miyazaki A5 Wagyu

Fact: Kuroge, Japanese Black, is one of the most famous breeds of Japanese wagyu because of its intense marbling. Miyazaki is one of the popular brands of wagyu alongside Kobe, Omi, Matsuzaka, Sendai, and Yonezawa. A5 grade meat is considered the highest ranking meat.

Pro tip: Enjoy the melt-in-your-mouth tenderness of the meat.
