Shumai 燒賣 烧卖 Shrimp and Pork Dumplings

Fact: Shumai, another popular dim sum dish, originated from Huhot between the Ming and the Qing dynasties. This dish has variations all over Asia including Indonesian siomay and Filipino siomai. (wiki)

Pro tip: One of the variations of shumai just requires adding a quail egg.
