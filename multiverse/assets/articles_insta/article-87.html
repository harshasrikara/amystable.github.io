Bánh Ít Ram (Vietnamese Steamed and Fried Glutinous Rice Dumplings)

Fact: The rice dumplings, from the central region of Vietnam, are filled with pork belly and mung beans and then topped with shrimp. The mochi-like dumpling sits on crispy-fried layer of glutinous rice which gives it a crunch.

Pro tip: You can add onions to it, and its eat with fish sauce.
