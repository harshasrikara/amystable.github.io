Squid Ink Cheesy Hotdog 핫도그

Fact: Called a hotdog, this popular Korean snack dish resembles a corndog. It consists of a sausage and chunk of mozzarella cheese skewered on a stick before being wrapped in a squid ink dough, breadcrumbs, and then deep fried to perfection. You get that perfect, gooey cheese pull when you take a bite.

Pro tip: Sprinkle a bit of sugar on it.
