Salmon Belly Sashimi 鮭ハラス

Fact: Like tuna’s toro, salmon belly is the fattiest, tastiest, and most prized cut of the fish. Sashimi-style sushi consists of eating the fish raw with a bit of wasabi and soyu without rice.

Pro tip: When eating sashimi, try not to overload the fish with soy sauce or extra condiments because that overpowers the flavors of the fish.
