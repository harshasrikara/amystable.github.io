Chinese Sizzling Beef 牛柳

Fact: This dish, served on a sizzling hot plate, releases delicious aromas after the sliced, succulent steak and onions are poured onto it. The steak is well-marinated, and the dish a bit of pepper to add to the flavor and aroma.

Pro tip: When selecting a cut of meat to use for this dish, choose ones that are good for stir-frying such as flank or skirt steak (Red House Spice).
