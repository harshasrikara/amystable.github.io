Bánh Bột Lọc • Vietnamese Clear Shrimp and Pork Dumplings

Fact: Originating from Hue, Vietnam, these chewy tapioca dumplings are stuffed with shrimp and pork. There is the bánh bột lọc trần (bare as seen) and bánh bột lọc lá which has the dumpling wrapped in banana leaves.

Amy’s note: If you sat next to me at lunch in elementary, middle, and high school, this was what I was having.
