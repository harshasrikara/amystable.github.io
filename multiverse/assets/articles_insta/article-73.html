Thịt Heo Quay 燒肉 Vietnamese/Cantonese Crispy Roasted Pork

Fact: This dish is often eaten at celebratory events such as weddings, but it can also be enjoyed on a regular day in both Vietnamese and Chinese cultures. The skin is crispy while the meat is savory, juicy and tender.

Pro tip: Add this to any dish, and it will be a game changer. I love eating it with vermicelli noodles or on rice. It could also be dipped in hoisin sauce.
