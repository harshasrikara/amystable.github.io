Galbi Jjim 갈비찜 (Korean braised short ribs) with jujubes, mushrooms, and carrots

Fact: Daegu, South Korea is considered the birthplace and home of the most delicious galbi jjim since it was first served in 1972. Dongin-dong district’s galbi jjim street is still famous for its delicious braised short ribs. (Wikipedia)

Pro tip: Eat the meat with a slice of kimchi and rice for added flavors!
