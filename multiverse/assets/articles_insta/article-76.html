Chạo tôm (Vietnamese shrimp on sugarcane), Nem nướng (grilled pork sausage), and Bò mỡ chài (ground beef sausage in caul fat)

Fact: These are popularly eaten as cuốn, wrapped in rice paper wrappers and herbs.

Pro tip: Don’t overstuff the rice wrapper otherwise it will be really difficult to rule. But if you’re ambitious, you can use two rice paper wrapper to stuff more.
