Balut / Hột Vịt Lộn / Fertilized Duck Eggs

Fact: This dish has its origins in the Philippines and has variations all over Southeast Asia, including Vietnam. Vendors buy the duck eggs and wait for it to incubated for 14 to 18 days before boiling them.

Pro tip: Being Vietnamese, I always eat my hột vịt lộn pepper, salt, and a side of rau răm, a fresh-flavored Vietnamese herb
