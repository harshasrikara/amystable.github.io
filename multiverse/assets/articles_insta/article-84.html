Dolsot Bibimbap 돌솥 비빔밥

Fact: Bibimbap consists of the mixing rice and various ingredients including gochujang, fermented bean paste, vegetables, eggs, and a protein. Dolsot bibimbap, a variation of this dish, is cooked in a dolsot or hot stone pot.

Pro tip: The best part of this dish, in my opinion, is the slightly burnt rice at the bottom of the pot which adds a nice crunch.
