Tsukemen つけめん Japanese Dipping Noodles

Fact: The idea of dipping noodles was a solution for slower noodle eaters who always had soggy, bloated noodles towards the end. With tsukemen, you have your noodles and toppings separate and then you would dip it into the broth.
