Crispy Pan Fried Ddeokbokki 떡볶이

Fact: This spicy rica cake dish is an incredibly popular snack dish in Korea, enjoyed in many variations including the cheesy ddeokbokki and seafood ddeokbokki. This particular one involves frying the rice cakes until they’re crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside before stir-frying them in gochujang.

Pro tip: You can add pepper flakes to make it spicier 🔥
