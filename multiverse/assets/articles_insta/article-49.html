Hủ Tiếu Đuôi Bò (Oxtail Soup)

Fact: This dish consists of a popular Vietnamese noodle soup called hủ tiếu and a particular gem, oxtail. The soup also consists of rice noodles, green onions, and in other variations, there are also some pork and shrimp.

Pro tip: Meat that is around the bone tends to be the most tender, so I particularly love oxtail. Don’t be afraid to pick it up with your hands and dig in.
