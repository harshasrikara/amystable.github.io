Salmon Sashimi Pizza (salmon belly sashimi, ponzu, and chili paste)

Fact: Sashimi is one of the several variations of sushi. The cut of salmon used in this “pizza” is the salmon belly which has a line of fat along its edges. This is considered one of the tastiest cuts of salmon.

Pro tip: When eating salmon belly or sashimi in general, don’t dip it in soy sauce right away. Take a bite to enjoy the pure flavors of the fish first.
