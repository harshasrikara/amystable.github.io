Nabeyaki Udon 鍋焼きうどん with Inari Sushi

Fact: Nabeyaki udon is cooked in an stone pot called a donabe. The udon noodle soup here is cooked with scallops, chicken thigh, an egg, dish cakes, tofu, and then shrimp tempura is added, but you can add whatever you want. On the side is inari sushi, a sweet tofu wrapper with rice inside.

Pro tip: Don’t overtook the egg you add to the soup. Leave the yolk runny so you can eat it with the udon noodles.
