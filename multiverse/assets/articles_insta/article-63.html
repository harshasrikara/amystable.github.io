Chaliapin Steak シャリアピンステーキ

Fact: This is the famous steak that was featured in the hit anime, Shokugeki no Soma (Food Wars). This dish’s signature is the usage of onions to tenderize the meat and was developed when Feodor Chaliapin requested an extra tender steak.

Pro tip: Use sautéed onions, and don’t forget to eat with a bowl of rice.
