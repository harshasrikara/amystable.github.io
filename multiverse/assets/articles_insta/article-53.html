Lẩu Thái Lan

Fact: This is a Vietnamese hot pot that is reminiscent of the bold flavors and spice of Thai cuisine. In the hotpot, you cook various seafoods as your protein and garland chrysanthemum leaves and cabbage as your vegetables.

Pro tip: Cook the seafood first and then put in the vegetables. This way, the flavors of the seafood is absorbed into the broth which will seep into vegetables to make them more flavorful.
