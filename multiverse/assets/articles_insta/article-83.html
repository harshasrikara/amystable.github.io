Taiyaki 鯛焼き 🐠

Fact: This dessert consists of a fish-shaped pastry filled with an azuki red bean filling. The pastry actually is in the shape of a sea bream, which in Japanese, is tai 鯛. This added to yaki, meaning baked gives the dessert its name taiyaki which means baked sea bream.

Pro tip: You can eat taiyaki with ice cream, and I especially putting a bit of ice cream onto the pastry.
