Khao Neow Dam Piak (“Thai Black Sticky Rice Pudding”)

Fact: This glutinous rice dessert is popular in various Asian countries, and therefore, goes by many names. The black sticky rice provides the dessert its earthly flavor and chewy texture which contrasts with the smooth creaminess and sweetness of the coconut cream paired with it.

Pro tip: For toppings, you can add a variety of things such as heart or palm (seen here) or even mango slices.
