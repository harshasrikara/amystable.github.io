Pork Satay

Fact: Thai pork satay is usually skewered marinated pork grilled to perfection, but here, the pork satay is served on mini baguette slices with peanut sauce, cilantro, cucumbers, and peppers.

Pro tip: Pork satay is always served with the sweet and savory peanut sauce, and licking the goodness of the sauce of your fingers at the end of the bite is even more delightful!
