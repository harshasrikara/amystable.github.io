Banh Xeo

Fact: Banh Xeo, literally meaning “sizzling pancake” because of the sound it makes while cooking in the skillet, is a savory pancake stuffed with mung beans, bean sprouts, shrimp, and pork.

Pro tip: When ordering Banh Xeo, ask for the bean sprouts to be left outside of the pancake. That way, the pancake doesn’t become soggy and remains crispy.
