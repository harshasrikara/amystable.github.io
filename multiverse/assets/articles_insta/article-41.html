Au Jus French Dip

Fact: This sandwich is made from sliced prime rib which is put between toasted French rolls. “Au jus”, meaning “with juices”, is the broth from the meat as it is cooked. It is then served along side the dish to be dipped in.

Pro tip: The bread is what absorbs all the broth dip and holds the broth so when you take a bite, you get the broth with the meat.
