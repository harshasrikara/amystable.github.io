Yakitori やきとり (Texas Kobe Beef, Pork Belly, and Chicken)

Fact: Yakitori originally refers to just skewered grilled chicken, but it has evolved to include a wide variety of things such as beef, pork, and vegetables. The food is skewered and grilled over a charcoal grill.

Pro tip: You can use a mini charcoal grill to keep the skewers warm while you eat. (as seen in the picture)
