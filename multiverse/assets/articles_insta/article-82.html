Mapo Doufu 麻婆豆腐

Fact: This tofu dish originated from the Sichuan province of China, a region famous for its spicy food. The tofu is cooked with minced meat in a spicy chili oil and a variety of herbs. It incorporates a style of numbing spiciness called má 麻 (numbing) and là 辣 (spicy hot).

Pro tip: Eat it with rice
