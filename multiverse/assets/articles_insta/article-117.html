Classic Katsu Curry (Fried Pork Cutlet Curry)

Fact: So I’ve talked about the introduction of curry to Japanese cuisine before, but did you know this dish is so popular in Japan that it’s considered the national dish?

Pro-tip: Bite after bite of curry can be tiring, and the flavors can grow monotonous. That’s why it’s often served with tsukemono, pickles. Eat some to add some sourness and layers to the flavor.
