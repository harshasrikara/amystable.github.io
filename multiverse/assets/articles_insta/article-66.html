Dim Sum 點心/点心

Fact: Dim sum is a style of Chinese cooking that is characterized by bite-size assortments of dumplings served in steamer baskets and pushed around in carts. Yum cha 飲茶/饮茶, the Cantonese word for “drinking tea” is now synonymous for dim sum because tea is often served with dim sum.

Pro tip: Drink tea to clean your palate.
