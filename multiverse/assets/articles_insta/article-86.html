Bún bò Huế (Vietnamese Spicy Beef Noodle Soup)

Fact: Bún bò Huế (BBH) originated in the ancient capital of Vietnam, Huế. This beef noodle soup arguably rivals pho in popularity, appealing to the spicy-loving crowd. It consists of vermicelli, beef brisket, lemongrass, herbs, and a pork knuckle.

Pro tip: You can add more chili oil to make it even spicier.
